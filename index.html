What We Do

Full-Service Auto Body & Beyond

From minor fender benders to full frame restorations — we handle it all with the precision of craftsmen, not just mechanics.

Collision Repair
Expert dent removal, panel repair, and structural restoration after accidents. We bring your vehicle back to pre-collision condition — or better.
Paint & Refinishing
From spot repairs to full resprays, our painters match factory finishes perfectly. We use premium materials for a deep, lasting gloss.
Frame & Structural
Advanced frame straightening and unibody repair using precision measurement systems. We restore structural integrity — your safety depends on it.
Custom & Restoration
Bring your dream build or classic car back to life. Custom paint schemes, body mods, and full restorations — we live for this work.
Clear Bra / PPF
Paint Protection Film installation that shields your finish from chips, scratches, and road debris — invisible, durable, and worth every penny.
24/7 Towing
Stuck on the side of the road at 2am? We've got you covered. Our towing service is available around the clock — call anytime.
